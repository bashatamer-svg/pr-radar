How it works

Your daily brand & reputation board

PR Radar watches the news for stories about Vodafone Egypt and its main competitors (Orange, WE, e&), reads each one, and lays out what matters today — and what to do about it. Here's how to read it.

📈 The pulse, at the top

A one-glance health check for the day.

Big number
How many negative Vodafone stories today. 0 with a green CLEAR badge means a calm day; a red ALERT means something needs eyes.
Competitor tiles
Orange, WE and e& — how much they're in the news and how many negatives, for context. This is market awareness, not your to-do list.

🗂️ The three lanes

Every story is sorted by what you should do about it.

Needs a response
Negative Vodafone stories — the ones worth addressing.
Wins to amplify
Positive Vodafone stories — good news worth boosting.
Market & noted
Everything about competitors, plus neutral items — useful context, no action needed.

🃏 Reading a story card

Each card tells you what it is, how big it is, and what to do.

Positive Negative
The tone of the story (also shown as the coloured badge).
Reach ●●●○○
How big / impactful the story is, 1 to 5. More dots = more people likely see it.
92% confidence
How sure the AI was about its read. Below 50% turns amber · low — worth a quick double-check.
◱ 2 outlets
How many outlets ran it. The more outlets, the bigger the story.
What to do
with this
Read (what it means) · Audience (who cares) · Action (the recommended move).
2h ago · By …
When it broke and who wrote it. Coverage expands the full list of outlets.

🔎 Filters

Two rows near the top narrow what you see.

Show
All, Needs response, Wins, Market — plus 📌 Pinned (team-flagged) and Low-conf (items the AI was unsure about).
Since
24 hours, 48 hours, Week or Month — the time window. On phones, swipe the rows sideways to see every chip.

📸 Share
Make a clean branded image of the story (or the whole pulse) to share.
📌 Pin · 🙈 Hide
Admins: flag a story for the team, or hide noise. Pinned items show under the Pinned filter.
